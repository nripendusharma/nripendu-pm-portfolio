# Government Digital Transformation

## 🏛️ Problem
- Low digital adoption across departments  
- Manual workflows causing delays  
- Lack of real-time data visibility  

---

## 🎯 Goal
- Increase digital adoption by 40%  
- Reduce processing time by 60%  

---

## 🧠 Approach
- Conducted stakeholder interviews across departments  
- Identified key bottlenecks in approval workflows  
- Designed a modular, scalable system  

---

## ⚙️ Execution
- Implemented workflow automation  
- Built dashboards for real-time tracking  
- Ensured cross-department stakeholder alignment  

---

## 📈 Impact
- 55% increase in adoption  
- 35% faster processing time  
- Improved transparency across systems  

---

## 💡 Learnings
- Stakeholder alignment is more critical than feature complexity  
- Simplicity drives adoption in government systems
