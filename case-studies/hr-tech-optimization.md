# HR Tech Optimization

## 🏛️ Problem
- Fragmented HR systems  
- Poor data visibility  
- Low candidate engagement/No show  

---

## 🎯 Goal
- Improve system efficiency  
- Enhance candidate experience  

---

## 🧠 Approach
- Mapped existing workflows  
- Identified inefficiencies  
- Designed unified dashboard  

---

## ⚙️ Execution
- Built centralized HR platform  
- Introduced analytics dashboards  
- Automated reporting  

---

## 📈 Impact
- Improved data visibility  
- Reduced manual reporting effort  
- Better employee engagement  

---

## 💡 Learnings
- Data clarity drives decision-making  
- UX plays a critical role in adoption
